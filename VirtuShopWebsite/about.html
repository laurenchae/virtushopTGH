VirtuShop - Who we are


Introducing VirtuShop* - the AR personal shopping app with the goal of “bringing the shopping to you”!
Allowing shoppers to have an in person shopping experience from home, this app allows you to have a virtual store at the tips of your fingers - browse through a
variety of stores, add items to your basket, and checkout for shipping or pick up from the store.
it works - users choose a brand to shop, scan their room/living space, and transform their room into a virtual store using AR.
